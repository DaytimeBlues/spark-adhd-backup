# Product Guidelines - Spark-ADHD

## Voice and Tone

Concise and direct: Minimizing distractions and overhead for quick interactions.

## Design Principles

- **Polished Home Screen**: Smooth and easily accessible logical home screen.
- **Sleek Bubble Interface**: Highly responsive, professional bubble interface.
- **Zero-Latency Performance**: No lag or hiccups allowed during interaction.

## Constraints

- **Vibe-Coding Workflow**: The project is managed by a non-developer/coder. Implementation and code management are handled by AI agents based on high-level 'vibes' and functional requirements.
